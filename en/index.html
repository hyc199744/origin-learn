ORIGIN Ecosystem — LGNS, Anubis Chain & On-chain Tools

A plain-language beginner's guide to Origin (ORIGIN): understand the core token LGNS, the self-built Anubis privacy chain, and (3,3) staking — then verify everything yourself with free on-chain tools. No background needed.

The three basics

What is Origin

A whole Web3 ecosystem: at its core is the token LGNS, plus staking, DAO governance and a self-built Anubis privacy chain. Think of it as an on-chain community with its own token and rules.

What is LGNS

The ecosystem's core token — an algorithmic token with 9 decimals (not 18). It uses the (3,3) game model that rewards long-term holding. Governance is a separate token, GLGNS.

What is Anubis

A self-built privacy chain, Chain ID 6714, ~2s blocks, with gas paid in DAI. Part of Origin's two-chain (Polygon + Anubis) setup.

The Five Trials — learn step by step

1 · Meet Origin

Get the terms straight: what Origin, Anubis and LGNS each are.

  • Origin = the whole Web3 ecosystem
  • LGNS = the core token (algorithmic, 9 decimals; governance is a separate token, GLGNS)
  • Anubis = a self-built privacy chain (Chain ID 6714, gas in DAI)

2 · Understand LGNS & (3,3)

Grasp what powers this token and why everyone talks about staking.

  • (3,3): a game model that rewards long-term holding
  • Staking rewards are protocol emissions every 6 hours (rebase)
  • High APY ≠ net profit — watch both price and real reserves

3 · Try Staking

From setting up a wallet to your first stake and claiming rewards.

  • Set up a self-custody wallet and safeguard your seed phrase
  • Learn where to buy LGNS and how to make a transaction
  • Start small, confirm it works, then scale up

4 · Join Governance

Know what Awake DAO is and how a proposal goes from idea to execution.

  • Awake DAO: where the community governs
  • Proposal → Discussion → Vote → Execution
  • Voting power comes from the GLGNS governance token & governance NFTs (not from holding/staking LGNS directly)

5 · Advanced & Security

Verify data on-chain yourself, spot phishing, protect yourself.

  • Use a block explorer to check contracts and transactions
  • Beware of new tricks like EIP-7702 approval phishing
  • Stick to official channels and verify on-chain yourself (DYOR)

FAQ

What's the difference between Origin, Anubis and LGNS?

Origin is the whole ecosystem, LGNS is its core token, and Anubis is the blockchain the ecosystem built itself. Origin is like a city, LGNS the currency in it, Anubis the ground beneath.

What is LGNS?

The core token of Origin — an algorithmic token (not a stablecoin), 9 decimals not 18, using the (3,3) staking model. Deployed on Polygon and Anubis. Governance uses a separate token, GLGNS.

What is Anubis Chain?

A self-built privacy chain, Chain ID 6714, ~2s blocks, gas paid in DAI.

Where do staking rewards come from?

They're protocol emissions every 6 hours (rebase). High APY ≠ net profit — check the price and real reserves, and verify on-chain yourself.

How do I stay safe from phishing?

Don't click unknown links, don't sign approvals you don't understand, review your wallet approvals regularly, and beware of EIP-7702 approval phishing. A real official will never DM you for your seed phrase.
